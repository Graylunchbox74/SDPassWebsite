About Us

SDPaSS is a community partnership designed to provide students with multiple pathways or “on-ramps” to quality employment through a credentialing system. This SDPaSS model uses meaningful, job-related experiences including internships, apprenticeships, certificates, associate and baccalaureate degrees to provide students with jobs, and businesses with well-trained employees. While the model can be replicated across the state and in many job categories, this initial SDPaSS will focus on three high demand areas: software development, network services and cybersecurity.

What We Offer

  • Supervised internships in business/industry -- supervised by faculty at Dakota State University or Southeast Technical Institute
  • Registered apprenticeships administered through the S.D. Department of Labor
  • Academic certificates in cybersecurity, network and security administration, or software development
  • Associate degrees in Software Support (SETI), Network & Security Administration (DSU), Software Development (DSU)
  • B.S. degrees in Cyber Operations (DSU), Network and Security Administration (DSU)
