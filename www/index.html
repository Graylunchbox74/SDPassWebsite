Community partnerships such as the SDPaSS ensure that structured work experiences and educational programs are reinforcing and relevant to workforce needs.

Why choose SDPaSS?

This SDPaSS model uses meaningful, job-related experiences including internships, apprenticeships, certificates, associate and baccalaureate degrees to provide students with jobs, and businesses with well-trained employees. While the model can be replicated across the state and in many job categories, this initial SDPaSS will focus on three high demand areas: software development, network services and cybersecurity.
